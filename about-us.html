Hello.........!!

My name is Hendy Saputra, born in Samarinda, July 10, 2001. I am the fourth child of four siblings, the fruit of the Jamil and Mila couple. Hendy is my nickname, I was born from a very simple family. I currently live in Jln. Otto Iskandar Dinatta, Gg Budiman rt. 23 rw.07, Samarinda, East Kalimantan. I still live with my parents and siblings. I have a hobby of football and organization.
